An entertainment streaming platform that aggregates live TV, radio stations, and movies from global sources in a single application.

- Stream live television channels in HD quality from around the world.
- Access live radio stations from global sources.
- Browse and stream movies in multiple languages and HD quality.
- Includes a movie recommendation system and comprehensive movie catalog.
